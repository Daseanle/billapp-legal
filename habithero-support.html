Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add a new habit?
A: Tap the "+" button on the main screen.

Q: How do I track my child's progress?
A: Navigate to the child's profile and view their habit completion history.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes, all data is stored locally on your device and protected with biometric authentication.
